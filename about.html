My Journey

I’m a junior at Washington University in St. Louis, transitioning from Physics to Mechanical Engineering. My passion lies in creating innovative solutions through 3D modeling, CAD design, and prototyping.

I’ve worked on projects like dye-sensitized solar cells to improve energy efficiency and built an automated humidifier control system using Raspberry Pi. As a Building Operations Manager at Truman State University, I honed my problem-solving skills, managing facility operations and safety protocols.

I’m also dedicated to inspiring others through STEM outreach, volunteering with programs like Girl Scouts to teach young students about science and engineering. My goal is to design impactful solutions that blend technical precision with creative ingenuity.
